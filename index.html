Software known as an inventory management system is useful for companies that run hardware stores, as it allows the store owner to keep track of purchases and sales. Customers who are dissatisfied, too much cash sitting in warehouses, and slower sales are all results of poorly managed inventory. This inventory management system can keep track of sales and available stock and advise a store owner on how much to buy and when to place a new order. A Windows application called Inventory Management System was created specifically for inventory control and produces all of the necessary reports for Windows operating systems.
This web-based software provides the owner with a plethora of features, including: 1. Maintain a record of every supplier who provides the goods (data editing, data deletion, and computation of the total pay amount). 2. Play a crucial part in maintaining the records of every employee in this organization (there are currently over 200 employees). Additionally, it generates a salary sheet automatically each month that includes the attendance and salary record. 3. It keeps track of every product in stock and alerts the owner in red. 4. It features an invoice system that captures all client data and allows the owner to examine and print customer history at any time.
